Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free initial consultation?

Yes. We offer a free initial consultation for all new enquiries so you can understand your options before making any commitment.

How quickly do you respond?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours on working days. For urgent matters, please mention it in your message and we will prioritise accordingly.

Do you work with clients outside of India?

Yes. We work with clients across India and with Indian businesses and individuals based abroad who need support with Indian law, taxation, and compliance.

What is your fee structure?

Our fees depend on the nature and complexity of the matter. We offer fixed-fee services for standard work and hourly or retainer arrangements for ongoing matters. We will always be transparent about costs before you commit.

Can I get legal help on WhatsApp?

Yes. For quick queries and to get started, you can reach us on WhatsApp. For formal matters, we recommend connecting via email or booking a consultation so we can give your matter the proper attention it deserves.
